Instructions for the Online Assessment

Welcome to the Online Assessment Platform! Please read the following instructions carefully before you start:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection before beginning the assessment.
  • Each assessment will have a time limit, which will be displayed on the screen.
  • Read each question carefully and choose the best possible answer.
  • Once you submit your answers, you will not be able to make changes.
  • If you encounter any technical issues, please contact support immediately.

Good luck!
